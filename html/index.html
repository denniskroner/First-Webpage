Neuruppin

This beautiful city on the picture above shows Neuruppin. Neuruppin is a small town with around 30,000 inhabitants. However, it is the 5th biggest city in Germany in respect of area.

Neuruppin is located at a huge lake around 70km north of Berlin and is the capital of the region Ost-Prignitz Ruppin.
